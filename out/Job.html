Class: Job

Job(jobId, sessionName, jobTemplate, isJobArray, jobArrayStartnullable, jobArrayEndnullable, jobArrayIncrnullable)

The Job class represents a job submitted to the DRMS. It contains various information such as the job's id, the session it belongs to, the job template that with the option used for its creation, and other info if the job is an array job.
